# 《自深深处》阅读记录

**日期**：2026-04-27  
**范围**：第四段5-8句

---

## 原文
> From the very first there was too wide a gap between us.  
> You had been idle at your school, worse than idle at your university.  
> You did not realise that an artist, and especially such an artist as I am, one, that is to say, the quality of whose work depends on the intensification of personality, requires for the development of his art the companionship of ideas, an intellectual atmosphere, quiet, peace, and solitude.  
> You admire my work when it was finished: you enjoyed the brilliant successes of my first nights, and the brilliant banquets that followed them: you were proud, and quite naturally so, of being the intimate friend of an artist so distinguished: but you could not understand the condition requisite for the production of artistic work.

---

## 翻译
从一开始，我们之间的鸿沟就太大了。  
你在中学时就很懒散，到了大学更是变本加厉。  
你没有意识到，一个艺术家，尤其是像我这样的艺术家，一个，也就是说，其作品的质量依赖于个性的强化，想要发展他的艺术，需要的是：思想的陪伴、心智的氛围、安静、和平以及独处。  
当我的作品完成了你会称赞它：你享受我首演的辉煌成功以及随之而来的盛大宴会：你很骄傲，这很自然，你是这样一位杰出艺术家的密友：但你不理解艺术家的作品得以产生的必要条件。

---

## 结构分析

### 第一句
`From the very first there was too wide a gap between us.`  
- 简单句，`there was` 存在句。

### 第二句
`You had been idle at your school, worse than idle at your university.`  
- 主句 `You had been idle`；`worse than idle` 是比较结构，相当于 `you had been worse than idle`。

### 第三句（长难句）

You did not realise
└─ that [宾语从句]
├─ 主语: an artist
│ ├─ 插入语: and especially such an artist as I am
│ ├─ 同位语: one, that is to say
│ └─ 定语从句: the quality of whose work depends on the intensification of personality
├─ 谓语: requires
├─ 状语: for the development of his art
└─ 宾语: the companionship of ideas, an intellectual atmosphere, quiet, peace, and solitude

### 第四句（四个并列分句，由冒号/分号和 `but` 连接）
- `You admire my work when it was finished`
- `you enjoyed the brilliant successes of my first nights, and the brilliant banquets that followed them`
- `you were proud, and quite naturally so, of being the intimate friend of an artist so distinguished`
- `but you could not understand the condition requisite for the production of artistic work`

## 核心词汇

| 词                    | 词性      | 符合文义的释义                                         |
| :-------------------- | :-------- | :----------------------------------------------------- |
| `intensification`     | n.        | 强化、增强（使个性更鲜明）                             |
| `personality`         | n.        | 个性、人格                                             |
| `companionship`       | n.        | 陪伴、交往（此处与 `ideas` 搭配，指“与思想的陪伴”）    |
| `atmosphere`          | n.        | 氛围、环境                                             |
| `solitude`            | n.        | 独处、孤独                                             |
| `brilliant`           | adj.      | 辉煌的、灿烂的、出色的                                 |
| `banquet`             | n.        | 盛宴、宴会                                             |
| `intimate friend`     | n. phrase | 密友、挚友（`intimate friendship` 是其名词形式）       |
| `condition requisite` | n. phrase | 必要条件、必备条件（`requisite` 为形容词，此处名词化） |
| `production`          | n.        | 创作、产生（此处指艺术作品的创作）                     |

## 注

- too wide a gap，很宽的鸿沟。想到鲁迅在故乡里面的那一句“**我们之间已经隔了一层可悲的厚障壁了**”。同样不在一个频道，一个是缺失某种东西，一个确实多了某种东西。
- 依然是不能用中文准确描述intellectual的一天。
- worse than idle，很久不接触这类比较级，倒是忘了比较级除了加er还有这一类。
- 其实Bosie也算的上是一个天才了。 他都had been idle at his school了，还能上university。
