# 《自深深处》阅读记录

**日期：** 2026-04-13  
**范围：** 第二句（整句）

---

## 原文
> Our ill-fated and most lamentable friendship has ended in ruin and public infamy for me, yet the memory of our ancient affection is often with me, and the thought that loathing, bitterness and contempt should for ever take that place in my heart once held by love is very sad to me: and you yourself will, I think, feel in your heart that to write to me as I lie in the loneliness of prison-life is better than to publish my letter without my permission or dedicate poems to me unasked, though the world will know nothing of whatever words of grief or passion, of remorse or indifference you may choose to send as your answer or your appeal.

---

## 翻译

> 我们那不幸的、及其令人悲叹的友谊，已经以我的毁灭和身败名裂告终；尽管如此，我们旧日情意的却常记忆伴我左右，而且，想到憎恨、痛苦与不屑竟要永远占据我心中曾为爱所居的位置，这令我无比悲伤：我想你心里也清楚：当我身陷囹圄孤独服刑时，给我写信会好过未经我许可发表我的信，或自作主张给我献诗——尽管世人永远不会知道你会选择以怎样悲伤、激情、悔恨或冷漠的言辞来答复或者鸣冤。

## 结构笔记

1. [主句1] Our ill-fated and most lamentable friendship has ended in ruin and public infamy for me
   [转折] yet
   [主句2] the memory of our ancient affection is often with me
     [并列] and
   [主句3] the thought is very sad to me
     [同位语从句] that loathing, bitterness and contempt should for ever take that place in my heart once held by love
       [定语] once held by love (修饰 that place)
   [冒号] → 引出对前文（loathing, bitterness and contempt）的补充/解释
   [主句4] and you yourself will feel in your heart that...
     [插入语] I think
     [宾语从句] to write to me as I lie in the loneliness of prison-life is better than to publish my letter without my permission or dedicate poems to me unasked
       [比较结构] A (to write to me...) is better than B (to publish... or dedicate...)
         [时间状语] as I lie in the loneliness of prison-life (修饰 to write)
         [方式状语] without my permission (修饰 to publish)
         [方式状语] unasked (修饰 dedicate)
     [让步状语从句] though the world will know nothing of whatever words of grief or passion, of remorse or indifference you may choose to send as your answer or your appeal
       [宾语从句] whatever words ... you may choose to send
         [定语] you may choose to send (修饰 words)
         [方式状语] as your answer or your appeal

## 注

1. 关于"ruin" 这个词，我认为是破产的意思，DeepSeek 说是毁灭，过往的翻译好像也没有提到破产，且当它是毁灭吧。
2. 关于 "I think"，这里的感情有点微妙。究竟讽刺对方惯会表演呢，还是嘲笑自己一厢情愿呢？鉴于前面没有提到失望，应该是讽刺。
3. “a strong feeling of……”, 这样的感情，好累啊。羡慕精力充沛的人。
