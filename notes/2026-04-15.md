# 《自深深处》阅读记录

**日期**：2026-04-15  
**范围**：第4-6句

---

## 原文

> If you find in it something of which you feel that you are unjustly accused, remember that one should be thankful that there is any fault of which one can be unjustly accused.  
> If there be in it one single passage that brings you tears to your eyes, weep as we weep in prison where the day no less than the night is set apart for tears. It is the only thing that can save you.  
> If you go complaining to your mother, as you did with reference to the scorn of you I displayed in my letter to Robbie, so that she may flatter and soothe you back into self-complacency or conceit, you will be completely lost.

---

## 翻译

如果你在这封信里发现了什么让你觉得自己被冤枉了，记住：一个人应该感激他的罪状里存在不实指控。  
如果信里有哪怕一个段落能让你落泪，那就像我们在监狱里——一个夜以继日用来流泪的地方——那就哭吧。这是唯一能拯救你的东西。  
如果你去找你母亲抱怨，就像你曾经因为我在给罗比的信里表露出了对你的轻蔑而做的那样，那么她自会哄你、安抚你，让你回归自满或自负的状态，那么你就彻底没救了。

## 结构拆解（固定格式）

### 第一段

[条件状语从句] If you find in it something
   │
   └─ [定语从句] of which you feel that you are unjustly accused (修饰 something)
         │
         └─ [宾语从句] that you are unjustly accused (feel 的宾语)
              │
              └─ [注] be accused of which → of which 提前

[主句] remember that one should be thankful that there is any fault of which one can be unjustly accused
   │
   └─ [宾语从句] that one should be thankful ...
         │
         └─ [状语/内容从句] that there is any fault (thankful 的内容)
              │
              └─ [定语从句] of which one can be unjustly accused (修饰 fault)

### 第二段

[条件状语从句] If there be in it one single passage
   │
   └─ [定语从句] that brings you tears to your eyes (修饰 passage)

[主句] weep as we weep in prison
   │
   └─ [方式状语从句] as we weep in prison
         │
         └─ [定语从句] where the day no less than the night is set apart for tears (修饰 prison)

[补充句] It is the only thing that can save you
   │
   └─ [定语从句] that can save you (修饰 thing)

### 第三段

[条件状语从句] If you go complaining to your mother
│
├─ [方式状语] as you did
│ │
│ └─ [介词短语] with reference to the scorn of you
│ │
│ └─ [定语从句] (that) I displayed in my letter to Robbie (修饰 scorn)
│
└─ [结果状语] so that she may flatter and soothe you back into self-complacency or conceit
│
└─ 注意：这里的 `so that` 不表目的，而是**自然结果**：“那么她就会……”
│
└─ [并列谓语] flatter and soothe
│
└─ [宾语+补语] you back into self-complacency or conceit

[主句] you will be completely lost

## 生词与短语

| 词                  | 含义                                                         |                |
| :------------------ | :----------------------------------------------------------- | -------------- |
| `with reference to` | /                                                            | 关于           |
| `scorn`             | *n.* [U] the feeling that someone or something is stupid, old-fashioned, or not as good as other people or things | 看不起         |
| `flatter`           | *v.* [T] to praise someone in an insincere way in order to please them or get something from them | 奉承           |
| `soothe`            | *v.* [T] to make someone feel calmer and less anxious, upset, or angry | 安抚           |
| `complacency`       | *n.* [U] a feeling of satisfaction with what you have achieved which makes you stop tying to improve or change things | 自满、安于现状 |
