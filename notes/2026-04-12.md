# 《自深深处》阅读记录

**日期：** 2026-04-11  
**范围：** 第一段第一句

## 原文
> After long and fruitless waiting I have determined to write to you myself, as much for your sake as for mine, as I would not like to think that I had passed through two long years of imprisonment without ever having received a single line from you, or any news or message even, except such as gave me pain

## 翻译

>在漫长而徒劳的等待后，我决定由我写信给你——为了你也为了我——因为我不愿去想，在我长达两年的监禁生活中，从未收到来自过你的哪怕一行字，甚至是消息或口信—— 除了那些给我带来痛苦的。

## 主干

**I have determined to write to you myself.**  
（我已决定亲自给你写信。）

## 生词与短语

- **fruitless** *adj.*  falling to achieve what was wanted, especially after much effort. 无结果的，徒劳的  
- **imprisonment** *n.* [U] the state of being in prison, or the time someone spents there. 监禁，坐牢
- **for one's sake** 为了某人的缘故  
- **pass through** 经历，熬过  
- **a single line** 哪怕一行字  
- **such as**（此处不是“例如”，而是“那种……的东西”）  
  *例：I cannot accept such gifts as would put me in debt.*

## 结构笔记

时间状语(After long and fruitless waiting)  
→ 主句(I have determined to write to you myself)  
    → 原因1(as much for your sake as for mine)  
    → 原因2(as I would not like to think that...)  
        → 宾语从句(I had passed through two years ...)  
            → 伴随状语(without ever having received...)  
            → 排除状语(except such as gave me pain)  <!--这里是as引导的定语从句，修饰前面的such-->

## 注##
最后一句中such的指代存疑。
DeepSeek 说在语法上，such指的是前面的a single line, news or message ，如果是这样理解的话，作者在狱中应该是收到了什么东西的，只不过全都不是他期待的好消息。
如果such指的是news or message，那就是没有收到对方的信件，但是有相关消息传入耳中。
