# 《自深深处》阅读记录

**日期**：2026-04-23  
**范围**：第四段1-4句

## 原文
> I will begin by telling you that I blame myself terribly.  
> As I sit here in this dark cell in convict clothes, a disgraced and ruined man, I blame myself.  
> In the perturbed and fitful nights of anguish, in the long monotonous days of pain, it is myself I blame.  
> I blame myself for allowing an unintellectual friendship, a friendship whose primary aim was not the creation and contemplation of beautiful things, to entirely dominate my life.

---

## 翻译
我将以告诉你我深深地责怪自己作为(这封信的)开始。  
我身着囚服坐在这间不见天日的牢房里，一个身败名裂的人，我责备自己。  
在辗转反侧的痛苦长夜，在漫长单调的痛苦白昼，我责备的是我自己。  
我责备自己，因为允许一种不健康的友谊——其主要目的不是创造和沉思美的事物——完全支配了我的生活。

---

## 结构分析（精确语法拆解）

### 第一句
`I will begin by telling you that I blame myself terribly.`

- **主句**：`I will begin`
- **方式状语**（by + 动名词短语）：`by telling you that I blame myself terribly`
  - `telling` 的间接宾语：`you`
  - `telling` 的直接宾语：`that I blame myself terribly`（宾语从句）
    - 从句主语：`I`
    - 从句谓语：`blame`
    - 从句宾语：`myself`
    - 从句状语：`terribly`

### 第二句
`As I sit here in this dark cell in convict clothes, a disgraced and ruined man, I blame myself.`

- **时间/伴随状语从句**：`As I sit here in this dark cell in convict clothes`
  - 从句主语：`I`
  - 从句谓语：`sit`
  - 地点状语：`here`
  - 两个并列介词短语修饰 `sit`：`in this dark cell`、`in convict clothes`
- **同位语**：`a disgraced and ruined man`（补充说明主语 `I` 的状态）
- **主句**：`I blame myself`

### 第三句
`In the perturbed and fitful nights of anguish, in the long monotonous days of pain, it is myself I blame.`

- **状语**（两个并列介词短语）：  
  `In the perturbed and fitful nights of anguish`  
  `in the long monotonous days of pain`
- **主句**：`it is myself I blame`（强调结构）
  - `it` 引导的强调句，强调宾语 `myself`。
  - 完整形式：`It is myself (that) I blame.`
  - 正常语序：`I blame myself.`
  - 被强调的成分提前，`that` 在文学/口语中常省略。

### 第四句
`I blame myself for allowing an unintellectual friendship, a friendship whose primary aim was not the creation and contemplation of beautiful things, to entirely dominate my life.`

- **主句**：`I blame myself`
- **原因状语**（for + 动名词短语）：`for allowing ... to entirely dominate my life`
  - 动名词 `allowing` 的宾语：`an unintellectual friendship`
  - **同位语**：`a friendship whose primary aim was not the creation and contemplation of beautiful things`（解释说明前一个 `friendship`）
    - 含定语从句：`whose primary aim was not the creation and contemplation of beautiful things`
    - 关系代词 `whose` 指代 `friendship`，在从句中作定语修饰 `primary aim`
  - **宾语补足语**：`to entirely dominate my life`（`allow ... to do` 结构）

## 核心词汇

| 词                    | 词性                                                         | 含义               |
| :-------------------- | :----------------------------------------------------------- | :----------------- |
| `terribly`            | *adv.* very, extremely                                       | 非常、极其         |
| `cell`                | *n.* a small room in a prison,where someone sleeps           | 牢房               |
| `convict`             | *n.* someone who has been proved to be guilty of a crime(犯罪) and sent to prison | 囚犯               |
| `be(public)disgraced` | *adj.* be made to feel ashamed, especially in public         | 耻辱               |
| `perturbed`           | *adj.* worried or annoyed because of something that has happened | 不安的、烦乱的     |
| `fitful`              | *adj.* happening irregularly（不规则的） for short periods of time | 间歇的、一阵阵的   |
| `anguish`             | *n.* mental or physical suffering caused by extreme pain or worry | 极度的痛苦、煎熬   |
| `monotonous`          | *adj.* boring because there is no variety                    | 单调的、千篇一律的 |
| `dominate`            | *v.* to have power and control over someone or something     | 支配、控制         |

## 注

- terribly，通常用来修饰形容词或副词，当用来修饰动词时，通常带着很强烈的感情，如及其痛苦地、及其严厉地。
- disgraced and ruined，身败名裂，表示当前的状态，前文中，ruin and publish infamy 也是身败名裂，表示事件的结果。
-  anguish和pain，中文翻译过来都是痛苦，但作者作为顶级文豪，本能地区分了其细微的差异以避免重复。
- unintellectual, 有点难。但这种不创造和思考美好事物的感情，无疑不健康。
